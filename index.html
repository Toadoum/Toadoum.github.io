About

I am a Multilingual NLP researcher and engineer focusing on low-resource language technologies, including machine translation, ASR/TTS, and discourse-level semantics. Currently a PhD candidate at CRIL (Université d’Artois), I am passionate about responsible AI, dataset design, and inclusive language technologies for the Global South. I have worked with organizations such as UNICEF, EPFL, and Batazia, and actively contribute to Masakhane and AfricaNLP communities.
